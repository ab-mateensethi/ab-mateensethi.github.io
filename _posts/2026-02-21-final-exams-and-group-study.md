At the start of this phase, we attended a Machine Learning workshop conducted by our Programming Fundamentals professor, Dr. Bilal Ahmed. In this workshop, we were introduced to practical skills and gained deeper insights into machine learning concepts. We also worked on hands-on activities, which helped us understand how these concepts are applied in real-world scenarios. After attending the workshop, I received a Certificate of Participation, which was a big achievement for me. It also helped me improve my skills and boosted my confidence in learning new technologies.

{% include gallery-trigger.html label="Preview Image" title="Workshop Certificate Preview" images="/assets/images/journal/gallery/article-07-photo-01.jpeg" captions="Machine learning workshop certificate." %}

As the first semester progressed, courses became more challenging and final exam preparation became the main focus. Python required regular practice of advanced topics, while Applied Physics experiments needed careful planning and practical understanding. The increasing workload taught me how to manage time, set priorities and stay focused on learning. To handle this, I joined a study group of six Computer Engineering students. Studying together helped us share notes, understand difficult concepts and solve problems more effectively. It also improved our teamwork, communication skills and reduced the stress of studying alone.

Upon completing the final exams, I reflected on my first semester with a sense of accomplishment. Achieving a GPA of 3.6 out of 4.0 was rewarding, but the greater learning lay in overcoming challenges such as time management, balancing lab work, assignments and hostel responsibilities. The first semester taught me resilience, discipline and the value of structured preparation. These experiences laid a solid foundation for my future semesters, giving me confidence to face new subjects, mentor juniors and continue building both academic knowledge and personal growth.

{% include gallery-trigger.html label="Preview Image" title="Semester Result Preview" images="/assets/images/journal/gallery/article-07-photo-02.jpeg" captions="Student DMC showing first-semester results." %}

Another important achievement during my first semester was in the ICT lab. My lab manual was ranked at the top among all Computer Engineering students of the 2025 session. My work was considered unique and well-organized, which made it stand out from others. Based on this, my ICT teacher selected my lab manual to be kept in the lab as a sample for future students. It will be shown to juniors as a reference to help them understand how to prepare their work effectively. This achievement was very meaningful to me, as it reflected my hard work, creativity and attention to detail.
